+# Trevecca Dining
+
+Dining at Trevecca is provided by Sodexo.  More information about the dining options on campus can be found at the [Sodexo Dining Website](https://trevecca.sodexomyway.com/en-us/).
+
+## Apple Dining Hall
+
+The Apple Dining Hall, located in the Jernigan Student Center, offers endless dining options for anyone on campus. A full salad bar, grilled and homestyle favorites, vegan and vegetarian options, a deli, desserts, and a designated allergy-friendly dining zone are available. Be sure to stop by and grab your favorites!

+The Hub is a downtown Nashville inspired restaurant located in the lower level of the Jernigan Student Center that caters to anyone who is searching for made to order grill experience. +## Special Dietary Needs
+
+For those who have special diet requirements due to medical reasons, the food service will work to accommodate your needs. A doctor’s outline of dietary needs is helpful. Inquiries regarding this process should be directed to the Associate Dean of Student Success and/or Coordinator of [Student Disability Services](mailto:disabilityservices@trevecca.edu).
+
+Trays are also available for students on the meal plan who are too ill to attend meals. A note from the resident assistant (RA) or resident director (RD) is necessary for this service.
