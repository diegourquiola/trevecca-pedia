# Academic Services
## (Tutoring Labs, One-on-one Tutoring, Academic Coaching, the Writing Center, Study Skills)

*In Academic Services, our mission is to provide support and resources to equip students to grow intellectually, socially, spiritually, and emotionally in order to be successful during their studies at Trevecca and beyond.*

The Office of Academic Services coordinates the following traditional undergraduate academic support programs.

- **Departmental Tutoring Labs** (traditional undergraduate students only)
- **One-on-one Tutors** (traditional undergraduate students only)
- **The Writing Center** (traditional undergraduate students and GRAE students)
- **Academic Coaching** (traditional undergraduate students only)

Use the links below to access more information about each of these programs, including REQUEST FORMS for both one-on-one tutors and academic coaches. Additionally, the section on “Study Skills Resources” provides research and links to a variety of study skills (e.g., time management, note-taking, actively engaging during lectures, memorization).
